Worst Tickets Ever

Green Bay Packers (29) / Carolina Panthers (32)

Date: Oct 3, 2005
Location: 35.2289, -80.8549 / Charlotte, NC 28202

Visited Charlotte in 2005 for the Monday Night Packers vs Panthers, and though it was a good time and good game, sat in the worst seats, I have had for any game. After the game, watched as one guy in a group of 10 was picking a fight with a homeless man who was asking for some food. One in my group of 3 intervened luckily without incident, and gave the man the remains of pre-game tailgate feast..........
