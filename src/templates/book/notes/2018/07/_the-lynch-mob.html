The Lynch Mob

(Pawns Are Disposable)

After being released from the Union County Jail, I walked my way out of town. While walking, I observed an unusual long line of the lynch mob speeding by, forcing me to walk in knee high uncut grass. Then I am faced with an open man hole, hidden by the uncut grass.

FAQ For The Future:

  • Who was the one who said, "He is walking toward a man hole"?

  • Who was the one who said, "It is a good idea if we uncover the man hole"?

  • Who was the one who uncovered the man hole, and did not ask why they weren't putting up any caution signs?

  • Did the lynch mob know thier origin story ?

  • Would it make a difference to them, if they did?
